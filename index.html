Projects

Ternak Insang | Mobile App

TernakInsang is a simple application designed for buying and selling koi fish. Unlike typical e-commerce platforms, TernakInsang focuses solely on one seller. In addition to facilitating koi fish transactions, the application also features articles providing education on various aspects of koi fish, such as proper care practices including selecting appropriate feed, feeding frequency, the use of cultivation tools, and medication usage. The educational section also includes articles covering news related to koi fish.

GoWell | Health Website

GoWell is a health control application for tracking and managing health lifestyle user. GoWell can connect user with doctor within the app. In addition to track user health record, GoWell also a features users can estimate their ideal body shape by entering their weight and height

Connect.ed

Connect.ed is an e-learning application for elementary school. With many interesting features and adjustments to the nature of elementary school children, Connect.ed could be a solution for elementary school children who are interested in opening the application and doing the assignments given by the teacher.
